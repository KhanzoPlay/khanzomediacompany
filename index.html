About

Founded in Tindivanam, Villupuram District, Tamil Nadu
Khanzo Media Company is a forward-thinking partnership-based business that focuses on empowering local businesses by bridging the gap between traditional retail and the digital marketplace. Our mission is to revolutionize the way local shop owners reach customers through innovative marketing solutions and streamlined logistics.


Our Key Platforms

Khanzo Play – A Digital Marketing Hub for Local Shops :

Khanzo Play is a unique Android app that transforms how local shop owners engage with potential customers online. Through our platform, we offer free advertising services, including the creation of promotional posters and videos for shops and products. These ads are published across various social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram, as well as within the Khanzo Play app itself.

In addition to digital marketing, Khanzo Play serves as a hub for exclusive discount coupons. Customers can subscribe to our app through a yearly plan (₹205.09 + 18% GST = ₹242) to access these deals. Subscribers also have the opportunity to refer others, earning a ₹50 referral bonus, which can be redeemed when their wallet balance reaches ₹500.

Khanzo Play further enhances the customer experience through Affiliate marketing. Our app features affiliate banners for products and services, allowing users to click through and purchase items from partner sites directly. Our affiliate links, organized by brand, offer users a wide range of choices, adding value to the platform.

Khanzo Mart – A Multi-Vendor Ecommerce Platform :

Khanzo Mart is our multi-vendor ecommerce site, designed to provide local shop owners a seamless way to list and sell their products online. What sets us apart is our seller-friendly model,

which includes:

* Zero seller commission

* Zero platform fees

* Zero packaging costs

Sellers can easily add their products to our platform and create iscount coupon codes. These codes are offered to customers via the Khanzo Play app, guaranteeing higher product visibility and sales. Additionally, Khanzo Media Company takes care of the entire logistics process, from product pickup to delivery, ensuring a hassle-free experience for both sellers and customers.

We currently serve the Pondicherry and Villupuram districts, with delivery times ranging from 1 to 3 days. The cost of delivery is passed on to the customers, while sellers focus on growing their business without worrying about logistics.

Our ecommerce platform also includes an open wallet feature, allowing users to add money to their accounts using a secure payment gateway. The wallet can be used for prepaid orders, enhancing customer convenience and loyalty.
